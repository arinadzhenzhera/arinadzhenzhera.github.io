My story

Become a friend — change the
world

I went to English Camp for the first time when I was 11. There, I found my first ever friends, and experienced acceptance and love. I found a family that I never knew I needed, and I decided to stay there. I started going there every summer, and, by the time I was 15, I knew for sure I wanted to work there. That was when I joined the Student Pedagogical Brigade “Student Training English Program.” The training was a year long. We studied not only basic theory of child psychology, but also medicine, game technology, and conflict resolution techniques. Having passed all the exams, I finally turned 16 and started working.

After working for a year, I had to take a break because I moved to a different country. Next summer I came back to the camp, and, when I had to leave, I knew for sure I have to start my own team and teach people myself. Join me! Together we can change the world by giving love to one heart at a time.
